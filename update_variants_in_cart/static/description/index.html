Update Variants In Cart

This app allows customers to change product variant attributes in cart itself.

If you don't have this app and your customer wants to purchase different variant of a product, then they will have to go through these procedure

  • First remove (by decreasing quantity) that product from cart (Now you've Remove button)
  • Then again visit that product's page
  • Select desired variant
  • Add it to cart again

This above process is tedious, time consuming and might just lose a sale for you.
