Key Features

Take a look at some of the key features of Kingfisher Theme.

Mega Menu

  • You can add products categories to this dynamic Mega Menu with the help of Odoo ecommerce website theme.

  • The Mega Menu is used to show selected product categories for navigation to offer an improved and navigation experience to the users.

  • You can manage up to five level navigations through this mega menu.

Brand Slider

With responsive Odoo webshop theme, You can add and display the brands just above the footer of your website home page by customizing and setting its placement according to the requirement.

Product Details Page

Display the details of the product along with the multiple images of the selected product. Also specify the order of the images to be displayed. Product label will be displayed when mouse will hover on multiple product image sliders.

Theme Customization

You can customize the Odoo business theme the way you want. You can change the theme layout, Theme's main color & Theme's font combination.

Header and Footer Customization

You can customize the Header and Footer the way you want. You can change the header and footer, main color & font combination.

Snippet for Blog Section

Display the latest blogs posted by your site or related to your products with Blog Slider Snippet provided by the html5 bootstrap theme for Odoo.

Banner Slider Snippet

With the Brand Slider Snippet display all the highly engaging brands from your store. Customize content and images for exclusive look & feel of the banner.

Product Slider Snippet

Add a snippet to your E-Commerce store containing selected products from product list. From this snippet your customer can directly add product to the cart and can also see ratings of the added product.

Category Slider Snippet

Add a snippet to your E-Commerce store containing products from selected product Categories. List Multiple Product Categories and add products to them. From this snippet your customer can directly add product to the cart and can also see ratings of the added product.

Multi Products Tab Slider Snippet

You can add products of multiple types into one single slider. There will be switchable tabs to display the products. You can add as many products as you want in that slider in numerous tabs.

Promo Snippet

Add promo products in your store with editable advertisements. Customer can directly shop for that product from the Promo snippet. You can also add URL for the text and set styles accordingly.

Brand Snippet

Display all the branded products that your store sells. Customer can directly shop for products of the available brands from the brand slider.

Single Banner without Slider

Display the most desired collection of your store with the single banner and highlight it for the store customers.

Testimonials Slider Snippet

Show the world what people have to say about your store with the Testimonial Slider Snippet.

Flipping Product Display Image

Customer can see front & back view of product. By hovering on the product image customers can see both the sides of the products.

Note: All the new features of Kingfisher v11 may not be available in older versions.
